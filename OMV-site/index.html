One More Voice

Lost Stories from the Britsh Empire


Overview of the Project

One More Voice is an open-access recovery project. We seek to find and publish narratives and other texts by individuals who lived as subjects with the confines of the British Empire, especially in the nineteenth century. Our site, which is now in its prototype phase, currently includes diaries, travel narratives, testimonies, letters, and autobiographical pieces.

Many such texts were once lost, buried in archives, or otherwise forgotten. Our project has identified them and started producing user-friendly versions of these works for the public. Ultimately, we hope easy access to the texts will transform how scholars, teachers, and others around the world think about the British Empire and about the stories that can be told about the Empire.
