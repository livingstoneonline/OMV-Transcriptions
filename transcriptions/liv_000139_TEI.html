Letter to Joseph Moore, 10 July 1874

Author(s) & contributor(s): Jacob Wainwright

Place(s) of creation: Kessingland Suffolk

Repository: National Library of Scotland (Edinburgh, United Kingdom)

Shelfmark / Identifier: Acc. 12444

Digital edition & date: One More Voice, an imprint of Livingstone Online, 2020

Critical editing & encoding: Heather F. Ball, Adrian S. Wisnicki

Note: This historical document, published in unabridged form, reflects the cultural distortions and prejudices of its time and may contain material that will upset or distress some readers.
