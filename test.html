Selim Aga
c.1826-1875  •  Wikipedia
Biography: Formerly enslaved person and traveler from Kordofan in the Sudan who was educated in Scotland, accompanied British explorer Richard Burton as a valent on various West African journeys in the 1860s, and produced accounts of both his life and travels.
“The Niger Expedition” (1858)
Co-creator(s): Anonymous
Format: Original letters, as combined, edited, and published in a British periodical
“Original Correspondence: West Africa” (1864)
Co-creator(s): Louis-Alexis Chamerovzow
Format: Original letter, as edited, contextualized, and published by a British writer in a British abolitionist periodical
